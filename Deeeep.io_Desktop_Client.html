What is it?

The Deeeep.io Desktop Client is a desktop client for deeeep.io(duh) built with ElectronJS!
You don't have to open up deeeep.io on your browser anymore!

Benefits


So currently there are a few perks, but the team will be adding more in the future!
The most used one is the built-in AssetSwapper, which allows you to assetswap by just entering the skin ID!
and btw, the app is actively under development :)

Safety


This question was asked by some beta testers, so I wanted to clear it up.
The client is completely safe for your computer, as we don't grab your personal information or anything really
