What is it?

The Deeeep.io Desktop Client is a desktop client for deeeep.io which gives you extra utility features.

You also don't have to open up deeeep.io on your browser anymore!

Benefits

There are several utility features that you can use, such as:

  • Assetswapper
  • Saving Screenshots
  • Multi-swap sprites
  • Pet-swapper
  • and more!

Safety

The client is completely safe for your computer, it doesn't grab your personal info or anything else

your operating system or browser may show a warning that it's unsafe but that is because it isn't issued with a certificate
